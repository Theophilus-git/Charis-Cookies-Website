How to Make Them:

  1. Cream butter and sugars – In a large bowl, beat the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.
  2. Add eggs and vanilla – Mix in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  3. Combine dry ingredients – In another bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Mix wet and dry – Gradually add the dry mixture to the wet mixture until combined.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips – Stir in the chocolate chips (or your chosen add-ins).
  6. Scoop and bake – Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 8–10 minutes, until edges are golden.
  7. Cool and enjoy – Let cookies cool on the tray for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
